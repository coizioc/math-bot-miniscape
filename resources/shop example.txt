This file is to show the syntax of the shop.txt file. This is simply a semicolon-delimited list with two parameters:

1) the item's id.
2) the quest required to purchase that item. If none, then this value is 0.

The price is 4 times the item's value.

=======EXAMPLE BELOW=======

73;0
78;4
